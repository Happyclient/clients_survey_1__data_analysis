Introduction

This dataset is a collection of responses to my survey that seeks to investigate the perception of clients on existing customer support channels in the telecom industry in Uganda, as well as their views on proposed solutions to some of the challenges therein. It was accomplished as a partial requirement to attain my bachelor's degree in computer science at Makerere University, in 2023.

In this notebook, i'll be answering the following questions:

Single variable (1d) questions:

  1. Which telecom service provider is considered the primary service provider by most respondents? go-to
  2. On which social media platform do most respondents have an account? go-to
  3. Which two social media channels are considered the best for sending a complaint to a telecom company? go-to
  4. Which three features are considered most essential by customers in a customer support platform? go-to
  5. Which factor is most important to customers when contacting customer support? go-to
  6. How comfortable are customers with texting a chatbot when receiving customer support? go-to
  7. How necessary is a platfrom dedicated to creating and managing customer support complaints to respondents? go-to

Multivariable (2d...) questions:

  1. What is the relationship between the respondents' primary telecom service provider and their contentment with the customer support services? go-to
  2. What is the relationship between the respondents' satisifaction with the customer support services offered by their primary telecom service provider and how necessary they find a web/mobile application dedicated to creating and managing their customer support complaints and issues? go-to
  3. What is the relationship between the factors considered when receiving support and the employment status of the respondents? go-to
